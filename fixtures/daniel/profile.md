# Daniel — Profile

Daniel is 48, Head of Operations for a homewares chain in the home counties, married to Caroline since 2006, father to Thomas (16). He has been in retail operations for twenty-six years; the pandemic was the defining stretch of his career and he knows it was the peak. The job is not bad — it is *used up*. Eighteen months ago, on a train back from Manchester, a fragment of fiction arrived in his head, and since then he has been quietly filling a black Moleskine in evenings, car parks, and the small hours. He has told no one. He doesn't yet know what the writing is, only that it has begun to feel like the most alive thing in his week.

## Architectural shape

This is a **mid-process Making emergence** with a hidden permission constraint. Making surfaces strongly (high stated, high felt_cost, quickening anticipation, daily writing, recent action) with `past_relationship: new` and `specificity: partial` — he doesn't know yet whether he's writing a short story or something longer, and he is protective of that not-knowing. Pull-quality resolves to `real` because none of the other branches apply: he is not saturated, the felt_cost is too high for phantom, his stated_strength is high enough to disqualify suppressed, and `direction_chosen: making` rules out behaviourally_divergent. Contribution surfaces in the `habit` quadrant with `pull_quality: ["saturated"]` and `pull_state: ["held_attributed"]` — the role is fully shaped and still moving but the man no longer reaches for it. Relationship surfaces `active` with `held_attributed` (Caroline, Thomas, Ben, the three golf men — all known shapes). The decisive cross-cutting output is **`mid_process`**, fired by the combination of an active direction and `recent_reaching: recent_and_awkward`: the eighteen-month-old reach is recent enough to still be awkward, and Daniel's own admission ("he has not yet found the language for it") is exactly what mid_process names. `held_unattributed` does *not* fire because the held thing — the Moleskine — is attributed (to Making) via specificity-strong relationship and contribution; this is a deliberate test of the negation rule.

## What this fixture tests

The fixture exercises (1) high-pull, partial-specificity, no-past-presence Making emerging cleanly as `real` rather than collapsing into `phantom_partial` (which Simon's fixture covers); (2) the `saturated` branch firing on a habit-quadrant Contribution where `preA` holds via `pull >= 30` rather than via `stated >= 50`; (3) `mid_process` firing precisely when an active direction co-occurs with `recent_and_awkward` reaching; (4) `held_unattributed` correctly *not* firing when other directions carry `held_attributed` despite `general_held_something: yes`; and (5) `permission_sub_shape: say_block` paired with `permission: 60` (partial band) — the architectural signature of a man whose action and want are both intact but whose ability to *speak* the new identity is constrained. Best-guess decisions, flagged here: Making `past_presence` set to `no` (his earlier business notebooks are not Making in the architectural sense); Growth `past_presence` set to `no` (he learned a craft but did not pursue learning as a direction); `recent_life_shape_change: no` (the 2023 contraction is over two years old and shape is otherwise long-stable); `capacity_strain: no` (he uses spare evenings, not strained slots).
